Note on XOR: A single McCulloch-Pitts neuron cannot represent the XOR function. This is because the points (0,1) and (1,0) that result in an output of 1 cannot be separated from the points (0,0) and (1,1) by a single straight line. This limitation is known as the problem of "linear separability" and was a key factor that led to the development of multi-layered networks.
